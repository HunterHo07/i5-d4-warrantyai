# WarrantyAI - Market Research & Analysis

## üìä Market Research Insights

### Consumer Behavior Statistics
- **70%+ of consumers** forget or misplace receipts within 6 months
- **Average household** owns 25+ items with active warranty/service coverage
- **85% of warranty claims** are never filed due to lost documentation
- **$2.9 billion** in unclaimed warranty value annually in the US alone
- **Growing demand** for personal asset management & digital ownership records

### Pain Points Identified
1. **Receipt Management Chaos**
   - Physical receipts fade, tear, or get lost
   - Digital receipts scattered across email accounts
   - No centralized tracking system

2. **Service Schedule Confusion**
   - AC filter changes, car maintenance, appliance servicing
   - Multiple reminder systems needed
   - Lack of proactive notifications

3. **Warranty Claim Complexity**
   - Complex claim processes
   - Missing required documentation
   - Unclear coverage terms

4. **Asset Visibility Gap**
   - No visual inventory of owned items
   - Difficulty proving ownership
   - Insurance claim complications

### Direct Competitors
1. **Bilt Rewards**
   - Focus: Receipt scanning for rewards
   - Weakness: Limited warranty tracking
   - Market Share: Small, niche

2. **Everdence**
   - Focus: Digital receipt storage
   - Weakness: No AI extraction or reminders
   - Market Share: Minimal

3. **Lemon**
   - Focus: Warranty tracking for electronics
   - Weakness: Limited categories, no AR/3D
   - Market Share: Growing but limited

### Indirect Competitors
- **Evernote/Notion**: General document storage
- **Google Photos**: Receipt photo storage
- **Calendar Apps**: Manual reminder systems
- **Insurance Apps**: Asset tracking for claims

### Competitive Gaps
- **No unified solution** covering all warranty types
- **Lack of AI-powered extraction** and smart reminders
- **Missing visual inventory** and AR/3D capabilities
- **No ownership verification** system
- **Limited integration** with e-commerce platforms
